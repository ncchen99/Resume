Tuckin — Cross-Platform Dinner-Matchmaking App

300 users Published on App Store + Google Play

Coffee Pocket — LLM Semantic-Search Café Platform

PWA
github.com/ncchen99/Coffee-Pocket Live Preview · tainan-cafe.web.app

An exploration tool themed around Tainan's cafés: use natural language ("a café with power outlets that's good for working") or practical tags to quickly find places that fit your situation—turning "finding a café" from ten-plus minutes of scrolling through posts into a few-second search.

  • LLM semantic-extraction pipeline: fetches café data from multiple sources, dedupes by Place ID, then uses an LLM to extract structured semantic tags from public reviews—each with a confidence score and evidence count (e.g., "plenty of outlets — mentioned in 12 reviews").
  • Natural-language query: a Supabase Edge Function parses colloquial requests into tag-filter conditions, supporting AND / OR combinations.
  • Community-correction mechanism: logged-in users can vote on tags to continuously improve data accuracy.
  • UI/UX optimization (core highlight): drawing on Google Material Design and Google Maps' "single-page + bottom sheet" interaction model, map, search, and store details are integrated into one screen with smooth transitions, achieving a zero-learning-curve, intuitive experience.
React 18 · TypeScript · Mapbox GL JS · Supabase (PostGIS + Edge Functions) · FastAPI · Python + Playwright · LLM (OpenRouter / OpenAI)

InkSync — Distributed Multi-Device E-Paper Control System

IoT Hardware + Software
github.com/ncchen99/InkSync

Use a phone app to control the displayed content of multiple e-paper devices at once—suited to exhibition signage, desktop info displays, and similar scenarios. Related experience in this domain also contributed to the First Prize / Innovation Gold Award at the 2025 E Ink e-paper innovation design workshop (team proposal "Skin-Brick E-Paper Building-Block Kit," in collaboration with NCKU Industrial Design's Human-Factors & Interaction Design Lab).

  • MQTT distributed architecture: both the ESP32-S3 firmware and the Flutter app are MQTT clients; each device maps to a dedicated topic by its MAC address, allowing an unlimited number of devices.
  • Image-processing pipeline: the app crops and uploads images to Cloudflare R2; the firmware downloads, decodes, and runs a dithering algorithm, outputting to a 4.0" 7-color e-paper display.
  • Real-time status & pairing experience: subscribes to device-status topics to show progress in real time; supports scanning the MAC barcode to pair a device.
ESP32-S3 · Waveshare 7-Color E-Paper · PlatformIO · Flutter + Riverpod · Mosquitto MQTT · Cloudflare R2
